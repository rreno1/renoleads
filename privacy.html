Privacy

Last updated: July 2026

RenoLeads uses this website to present land-lot listings and receive inquiries. This page describes the information the site may collect when you browse or contact us.

Information you provide

If you submit an inquiry, the form may collect your name, mobile number, email address, property interest, preferred contact method, preferred date, and message. We use this information to respond to your request and coordinate a conversation or site visit.

Local device storage

Saved lots and recently viewed listings are stored in your browser on the current device. They are not a substitute for an account and may be cleared when browser storage is cleared.

Analytics and service providers

The site may use Firebase services for published property records, lead submissions, and analytics when valid project configuration is present. When Firebase is not configured or unavailable, inquiries are buffered locally and the form tells you that the submission was not sent to a live database.

Property information

Listing descriptions, photos, map references, pricing, and document notes should be treated as information to review, not as a guarantee. Verify ownership, boundaries, access, documents, terms, and other material facts with appropriate professionals before making a decision.

Contact

For questions about this notice or a listing, use the contact form or a configured direct contact channel shown on the site.
