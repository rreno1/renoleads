How to Buy Land Lots in Polomolok

Our step-by-step guide from initial property selection to holding your clean TCT land title.

1

Select & Evaluate Your Property

Browse available land parcels on RenoLeads. Compare lot areas (300 sqm to 1,200+ sqm), barangay locations, total price, and document status.

2

Book a Guided Site Inspection

Schedule a convenient date for an actual site viewing. Our accredited real estate representative will accompany you, showcase lot boundaries, and road access.

3

Lot Reservation & Payment Terms

Reserve your chosen lot with a low reservation fee. Select your preferred payment plan: Spot Cash Discount or Flexible Monthly Installments.

4

Contract Signing & Title Transfer

Sign the Contract to Sell / Deed of Absolute Sale. Upon full settlement, receive your notarized Deed of Sale and official TCT land title under your name.
