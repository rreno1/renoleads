Why Invest in Polomolok, South Cotabato?

Polomolok is the agricultural & commercial powerhouse of South Cotabato, offering high land value growth, cool mountain climate, and strategic access to General Santos City.

High Growth Investment Zone

Cool Climate & Scenic Views

Situated right at the foot of Mount Matutum, Polomolok enjoys fresh mountain breezes and cooler temperatures than nearby coastal cities—making it the ideal location for retirement homesteads and vacation farm houses.

High Value Growth Factors

Land values in Polomolok are influenced by expanding residential subdivisions, industrial developments, and commercial bypass roads connecting directly to GenSan. Buyers should conduct independent due diligence.

Fertile Volcanic Soil

Home to Dole Philippines' world-renowned pineapple plantations. The volcanic soil of Polomolok is exceptionally rich, ideal for growing fruit trees, vegetables, and high-value crops on homestead lots.
